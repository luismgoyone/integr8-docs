# First Steps Overview

Integr8 tracking platform offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to streamline affiliate marketing and enhance security through advanced fraud detection mechanisms.

[  ![ad tracking](https://24metrics.getoutline.com/api/attachments.redirect?id=35120b45-b613-47df-a8f1-08c82c682606)](https://integr8.co/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Tracking-1.png)

Here's an overview of its key features:

## Organization Management

Efficiently structure your team by defining roles and permissions, ensuring that each member has appropriate access levels. This hierarchical setup promotes operational efficiency and data security.

## Affiliate Dashboard

Provide affiliates with a [white-label dashboard](https://integr8.co/docs/articles/affiliate-dashboard/ "Custom publisher signup dashboard") where they can access statistics, explore offers, generate tracking links, and configure postbacks. This customization fosters stronger partnerships and brand consistency.

## User Management

Manage user accounts effectively by assigning specific roles and permissions, ensuring that team members have access only to the information pertinent to their responsibilities.

Tracking Platform Setup

Implement global tracking by configuring integrations, postback URLs, and custom domains. This setup enables precise tracking and analytics, essential for informed decision-making.

## Billing

Customize billing details, including company information, invoicing emails, VAT numbers, and currencies. The platform supports flexible subscription plans tailored to diverse business needs.

## Advertiser Setup

Initiate ad campaign tracking by creating advertiser profiles. This foundational step allows for organized offer management and the application of specific rules or targeting criteria.

Integr8's platform integrates these features to provide a robust solution for affiliate marketing, emphasizing security, customization, and comprehensive tracking capabilities.
