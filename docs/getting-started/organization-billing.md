## Introduction

Login to your **Integr8** account and Navigate to *Organization -> Settings*. Access the Settings TAB where you may edit and update your ***billing details***:

* Invoice Details
* Card Payment
* Partner Login
* Custom SMTP

 ![billing](https://24metrics.getoutline.com/api/attachments.redirect?id=6f2573fb-d2a9-4feb-a168-88cad0f18449)

## Invoice Details

Includes the company name, billing address, the invoicing email, the VAT number, the currency and he business type. These details can be eventually edited and updated at any time.

## Card Payment Details

The Integr8 subscription plans are always connected to a credit card that will be used every time a new payment is due. The card is usually added and saved during signup but t can be eventually changed later on as well.

## Partner Login

Create a white-label dashboard for publishers to sign up and access offer tracking links and statistics. For more details and how to set up a white label dasboard for affiliates, [click here](https://integr8.co/docs/articles/affiliate-dashboard/ "Affiliate Dashboard Access").

## Custom SMTP

Use a custom SMTP server to send email using your company details. If not set, by default, the system will use the Integr8 meail notifier.
