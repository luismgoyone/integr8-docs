## Introduction

With Integr8 platform you may group and filter the results in many ways using the Reports section of the platform. In addition to that, you may also export a specific report in a CSV format. The exported reports will be available in *Reports -> Download Center* section for 90 days.

## Where to find it?

To download the exported report, go to ***Reports*** *--> Download Center*\*\* and select the report from the list.

 ![download center](https://24metrics.getoutline.com/api/attachments.redirect?id=5e02c371-e27f-40c0-830f-9ca49c64ded2)

## What data can I export?

You can export Overview Reports, Clicks, Conversions and Events Reports which you can find under the Reports menu label.

After generating the reports, you can click the Export as CSV button. Now the download of the report is submitted and can be seen as a download request in the Download Center. You can download the report there, after the request's status turns into COMPLETED.

## What to consider?

* The report is limited to maximum 5 million rows
* The retention period of each file is 90 days
* You are able to submit a maximum of 10 requests a day
