## Introduction

Global offer Revenue and Payout can be set on offer level in each Offer settings. For Events Revenue and Payout, [create the Event](https://integr8.co/docs/articles/events-tracking/ "Events Tracking") first in the same section. However, it is also possible to define [custom payouts](https://integr8.co/docs/articles/custom-payouts/) on Publisher level for any offer.

## Global Revenue and Payout

* Navigate to ***Offers***
* Select and offer and click on ***Revenue & Payout TAB***
* Edit *Conversion* or an *Event* global revenue and payout
* Optionally, turn on the switch to *Allow Duplicate Conversions and/or Events,* it will allow multiple conversions and events with the same Transaction ID.
