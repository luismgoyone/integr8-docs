# Offer setup for tracking

Integr8.co offers a comprehensive suite of tools designed to optimize affiliate marketing campaigns, ensuring effective tracking, precise targeting, and robust fraud prevention. Here's an overview of their key features:

## Offer Setup and Management

* Offer Configuration: Easily set up offers by defining essential parameters such as revenue, payouts, and cappings to align with your campaign objectives.
* Landing Pages: Assign specific landing pages to offers, enhancing user engagement and conversion rates.
* Targeting Options: Utilize advanced targeting based on geographic location, device type, and other criteria to reach your desired audience effectively.

## Tracking and Analytics

* Affiliate Link Tracking: Generate standard or JavaScript-enhanced tracking links to monitor affiliate performance accurately.
* Conversion and Event Tracking: Implement server-to-server (S2S) postback URLs to track conversions and subsequent events, providing detailed insights into user actions.
* Custom Payouts: Set tailored payout structures for individual publishers or events, offering flexibility in commission strategies.
* Blocked Sub-IDs: Exclude specific sub-IDs to prevent unwanted traffic sources, maintaining the quality of your campaigns.

## Fraud Detection and Prevention

* Integrated Fraud Prevention: Activate real-time fraud screening at various levels—clicks, conversions, impressions, and events—to safeguard your campaigns.
* User Fingerprinting: Employ advanced JavaScript-based user fingerprinting to enhance fraud detection capabilities.

By leveraging these features, Integr8.co empowers businesses to streamline their affiliate marketing efforts, ensuring efficient tracking, targeted outreach, and protection against fraudulent activities.
